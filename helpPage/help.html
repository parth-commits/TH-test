Instructions on how to
setup:
Start by scanning the QR code
on your Rewards Card with a phone camera.
You will get 86 alpha-
numeric characters seperated
by 2 vertical bars ("|") into 3 groups. Ex:
0123456789012345678901 |
0123A4B5C678D90E1F2G3456H7I89012 |
AB01234C567890DE12F3G456H7IJ8901
Note these 3 groups of
numbers down.
Now go back from this page
and into the page. There,
enter the 3 groups of characters. Once done, press to save it.
Now whenever you want to scan the rewards card, press
from homescreen to see
QR code.
